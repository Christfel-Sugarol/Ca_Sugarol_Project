How did I come up with this topic?

Teenage pregnancy is one of the world problems. Especially for poorer countries like Philippines, the problem is larger. Because of that you can more or less encounter some of your acquaintances to experience teenage pregnancy. In my life I have seen some of my acquaintances becoming a parent while they are a teenager. There's also some of them with my relatives and I see that they're not so well-off in life. Which is why I'm wary about teenage pregnancy and chose this as my topic for this vlog.

What is my hope for this issue?

What is my hope for this issue? While other well-off countries have small amount of teenage parents. The rest of the countries have lots of them. Teenage pregnancy is related to the country's state and there's nothing we can do about that unless the countries themselves change. But at least I hope that teenage parents are treated better in society. Pushing them around because of a mistake would make their states worse that before. I hope that everyone will start caring more about these children rather than hating and avoiding them
